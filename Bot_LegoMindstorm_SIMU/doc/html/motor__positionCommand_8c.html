int32  Decel

Create a trajectory for one motor with a trapezoidal speed curve.

The trajectory start with a speed=0 then accelerate at a rate equal to 'Accel' until it reaches speed='VMax' before the end of the path the wheel decelerate with a rate of 'Decel' then stop.

Parameters
motorId number of motor on wich to apply the trajectory
out_commandResult of the computation
distDistance to run in meters
VMaxMaximum speed at the wheel in meters/second
AccelAcceleration in meters/second^2
DecelDeceleration in meters/second^2
